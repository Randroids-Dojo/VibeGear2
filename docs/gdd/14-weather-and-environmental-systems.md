# 14. Weather and environmental systems

## Weather types

- Clear
- Overcast
- Light rain
- Heavy rain
- Fog
- Snow
- Night
- Dusk / neon rain

## Visual effects

- Rain streaks and road sheen
- Snow particles and soft roadside whitening
- Fog draw-distance fade
- Headlight-like bloom pools at night
- Heat shimmer in desert tours
- Tunnel adaptation shifts

## Physics effects

- Rain and snow reduce base grip
- Heavy weather increases collision risk by lowering visibility
- Fog does not change grip directly but changes reaction time
- Night slightly increases danger by reducing read distance

## Track-specific weather

Every track supports 1 to 3 approved weather sets, not all conditions. This preserves authored readability and avoids impossible combinations.

## Forecasting in pre-race UI

The pre-race card should show:

- Current condition
- Surface temperature band
- Recommended tires
- Grip rating
- Visibility rating

## Accessibility options

- Weather particles intensity slider
- Reduced glare mode
- High-contrast roadside signs
- Fog floor clamp for readability
- Flash reduction for lightning/night bloom
