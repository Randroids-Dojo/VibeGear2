# 24. Content plan

## MVP content

- 1 car class with 3 starter cars
- 2 tours, 8 tracks
- Clear/rain/fog weather
- Championship, quick race, practice
- Basic garage, repairs, upgrades
- Local save
- Keyboard support
- Core AI field
- Time trial without ghost share

## Vertical slice

The vertical slice should prove the whole loop:

- 1 full four-race tour
- 1 garage flow
- 1 weather-dependent tire decision
- 1 late-race damage recovery story
- 1 nitro-driven overtake moment
- 1 original music theme
- 1 original pseudo-3D renderer

## Full v1.0 content

- 8 tours
- 32 tracks
- 6 playable cars
- 8 upgrade lines
- 7 weather/visibility profiles
- Championship, quick race, time trial, practice
- Ghost save/load
- Online leaderboard
- Track mod loading
- Accessibility settings

## Stretch content

- Daily challenge
- Split-screen local multiplayer
- Reverse tracks
- Route forks
- Community playlist mode
- Dynamic sponsor system
