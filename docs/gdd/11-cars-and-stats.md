# 11. Cars and stats

VibeGear2 should use fictional production archetypes rather than licensed analogs.

### Car classes

| Class | Role | Strength | Weakness |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sprint | Beginner friendly | Grip, recovery, low repair cost | Lower ceiling |
| Balance | All-purpose | Reliable in all tours | No dominant edge |
| Power | Expert speed | Top speed, nitro scaling | Harder in weather |
| Enduro | Damage/tour specialist | Durability, stable late race | Slower launch |
| Wet-spec | Niche unlock | Excellent in rain/snow | Weak in dry finals |

### Stat categories

- Top speed
- Acceleration
- Grip
- Stability
- Durability
- Nitro efficiency
- Repair cost class
- Weather affinity

### Balance rules

- No car should be dominant in all weather.
- Repairs and setup affinity should offset raw pace.
- Starter cars must be championship-viable through at least the midgame.
- Late cars unlock mastery; they do not replace player skill.
