# 8. World and Progression Design

## Official v1.0 Campaign Size

| Item | Target |
|---|---:|
| Regions | 8 |
| Races per region | 4 |
| Total official tracks | 32 |
| Optional bonus tracks | 8 |
| Total campaign duration | 3-5 hours first completion |
| Completionist duration | 8-15 hours |

This target is intentionally smaller than the 64-race source-game structure to remain practical for an open-source solo or small-team project.

## Unlock Structure

```text
Tutorial Sprint
 -> Region 1 unlocked
 -> Complete 4 races and qualify overall
 -> Region 2 unlocked
 -> Continue through Region 8
 -> Finale race unlocked
 -> Hard mode, mirror tours, and community challenge filters unlocked
```

## Tour Completion

A region is completed when:

- The player qualifies in all 4 races.
- The player earns at least 6 total tour points, or finishes top 10 in all races.
- The player is not in unpaid critical repair debt.

## Difficulty Ramp

| Region | Main New Pressure |
|---|---|
| 1. Neon Harbor | Basic steering, traffic, boost. |
| 2. Redwood Circuit | Longer curves, mild fog, off-road punishment. |
| 3. Mirage Basin | Heat haze, blind crests, wide fast turns. |
| 4. Storm Coast | Rain, wet grip, puddles, visibility changes. |
| 5. Alpine Static | Snow edges, narrow lanes, elevation. |
| 6. Glass Canyon | High speed, barriers, steep descents. |
| 7. Metro Midnight | Night traffic, tunnels, tight chicanes. |
| 8. Aurora Highway | Mixed weather, long races, elite rivals. |

## Thematic Variety

| Region | Visual Theme | Weather Bias | Track Feel |
|---|---|---|---|
| Neon Harbor | Neon docks, signs, cranes, skyline | Clear, night rain | Wide roads, intro curves |
| Redwood Circuit | Forest, sawmills, fog, bridges | Fog, drizzle | Medium curves, visibility |
| Mirage Basin | Salt flats, desert towns, mirage shimmer | Heat, dust | High-speed straights |
| Storm Coast | Ocean roads, lighthouses, storm walls | Rain, storm | Wet braking, puddles |
| Alpine Static | Snowy passes, radio towers, icy shoulders | Snow, fog | Narrow, technical |
| Glass Canyon | Crystal cliffs, tunnels, red rock | Clear, wind | Elevation, barriers |
| Metro Midnight | Arcades, overpasses, tunnels | Night, rain | Dense traffic, tight turns |
| Aurora Highway | Polar roads, observatory, aurora sky | Snow, clear night | Long mastery tracks |
